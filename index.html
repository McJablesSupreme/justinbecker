ABOUT ME

I'm a junior programmer analyst with over a decade of experience in education, healthcare, and customer service.

I've also earned both my midrange programming certification from MCC and my associate application developer certification from COMMON.

I won 1st place in the student RPG programming competition at the WMCPA iCON 2024 IBMi conference, and I received MCC's 2024 award for excellence in software development.

I had the honor of being featured on all of MCC's social media pages as one of their spotlight 2024 graduates.
